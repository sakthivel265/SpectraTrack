Spectratrack

Our IoT Tracking System is an innovative solution designed to provide real-time monitoring and enhanced security for valuable assets. Built around the powerful ESP32-WROOM module for GPS tracking and the ESP32-CAM for image capture, the system combines precise location data with visual verification to deliver a comprehensive tracking experience. The core functionality of the system revolves around its ability to pinpoint the exact location of an object while simultaneously capturing images when necessary. This ensures that users not only know where their asset is, but can also see what is happening around it. Whether it is a vehicle, shipment, or personal belonging, the combination of GPS and camera data provides a higher level of situational awareness and security. Data collected by the device is transmitted over the internet, making it accessible from anywhere in the world through intuitive web and mobile interfaces. This ensures constant connectivity and the ability to respond quickly to any movement or security concern. The portability of the device means it can be deployed in various environments without requiring complex installation.

Applications

  • Logistics & Supply Chain - Monitor the real-time location and condition of shipments to ensure timely deliveries and prevent loss.
  • Fleet Management - Track vehicles and drivers for improved route planning, operational efficiency, and safety.
  • Asset Protection – Safeguard valuable equipment, tools, or electronics from theft or misuse.
  • Personal Security – Track personal belongings or even monitor the location of loved ones for safety purposes.
  • Event & Field Operations – Use during outdoor events, expeditions, or construction projects to monitor movement of resources and personnel.
